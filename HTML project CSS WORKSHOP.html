My babies

Kahlouna is my 8 years old baby. On march 2016, I just woke up to find that a cat gave birth on my blanket : 5 little angels. Every little one was adopted but no one was interested in the black cat. So I knew I had to keep her. Since then, she is my best buddy.



I saved her from an abusive kid in 2019. But I think she saved me. This cat probably had trust issues and mommy issues combined but I will go to the moon and back for her, no questions asked.
